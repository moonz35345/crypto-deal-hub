Your 12-Point Security Checklist & Beginner Roadmap

Security First - The 12-Point Checklist

  1. Never share your seed phrase. Not with anyone, not ever. No legitimate service will ask for it.
  2. Use a hardware wallet for >$500. Ledger or Trezor for significant holdings.
  3. Enable 2FA everywhere. Use Authy or Google Authenticator, NOT SMS.
  4. Verify URLs twice. Bookmark your exchanges. Watch for typosquatting (krakennn.com).
  5. Test small withdrawals first. Always send $10 before moving $1000.
  6. Use dedicated devices/VMs. No random downloads on your trading machine.
  7. Separate hot/cold storage. Trading funds separate from long-term holdings.
  8. Check contract addresses. Copy-paste, then verify on Etherscan.
  9. Beware "guaranteed returns". If it sounds too good to be true...
  10. Never sign random wallet messages. "Approve" can drain your entire wallet.
  11. Use different passwords per exchange. Password manager required.
  12. Keep records for taxes. Every trade, every withdrawal, every swap.

Wallet Setup Priority

  • Beginner: Start with exchange wallets → MetaMask → Ledger
  • Intermediate: MetaMask + Ledger combination
  • Advanced: Multiple hardware wallets + multisig for large amounts
Pro Tip: Buy a Ledger first, BEFORE you buy crypto. This prevents the "I'll secure it later" trap where you lose funds.

Tax Planning Basics

  • US: FIFO accounting, 30% max capital gains rate
  • UK: 10-20% CGT allowance, pooled accounting
  • EU: Varies by country, generally 30% flat or progressive
Important: Keep all transaction records. Use portfolio trackers like CoinTracker or Delta.
