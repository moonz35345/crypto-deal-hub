Platform Reviews

Every platform is tested hands-on. We evaluate fees, security, coin selection, user experience, and customer support — then give you our honest verdict.

K

Kraken

Centralized Exchange · Est. 2013
4.7 ★
The most well-rounded exchange we've tested. Kraken has never suffered a major security breach since 2013 — a remarkable track record. With 400+ coins, proof-of-reserves audits, and customer support that responds in under 4 hours, it's our top pick for most users.
  • No major security breach since founding
  • Proof of reserves independently audited
  • Support responds in under 4 hours
  • 400+ cryptocurrencies available
  • Base trading fees (0.16%/0.26%) slightly higher than MEXC
  • Bank transfers can take 1-3 days
Our take: Best overall exchange for most users. The gold standard for security and reliability. Start here if you're new to crypto.

M

MetaMask

Browser Wallet & DeFi Gateway · Free
4.4 ★
The most popular Ethereum wallet with 30M+ users. MetaMask connects to virtually every dApp and DeFi protocol. Available as a browser extension and mobile app, it's the essential gateway to the decentralized web.
  • 30M+ active users — industry standard
  • Universal dApp compatibility
  • Pair with a hardware wallet for security
  • Free to use, open-source
  • Vulnerable to phishing if used without hardware signer
  • No built-in fiat on-ramp
Our take: Essential for Ethereum and EVM DeFi users. Pair with a hardware signer for maximum security. See our wallet comparison guide.
Get MetaMask → Affiliate link
L

Ledger

Hardware Wallet · $79-$249
4.8 ★
The gold standard for cold storage. Ledger's EAL6+ certified secure element keeps your private keys offline and immune to computer viruses. Supports 5,500+ assets and integrates with MetaMask for DeFi access.
  • EAL6+ certified secure element chip
  • 5,500+ supported assets across all major chains
  • Integrates with MetaMask for DeFi
  • Bluetooth, USB-C, and NFC connectivity
  • Premium price ($79 for Nano S Plus, $249 for Flex)
  • Ledger Recover service raised privacy concerns in 2023
Our take: Best hardware wallet, period. Worth every cent for securing significant holdings. Pair with MetaMask for the complete setup.
Visit Ledger → Affiliate link
C

Coinbase

Beginner-Friendly Exchange · Est. 2012
4.2 ★
The easiest on-ramp for complete beginners. Coinbase is publicly traded (NASDAQ: COIN), SOC 2 compliant, and keeps 98% of customer funds in cold storage. The "Coinbase Earn" program pays you small amounts of crypto to learn about different projects.
  • Easiest fiat on-ramp for beginners
  • Publicly traded company — full financial transparency
  • "Coinbase Earn" pays you to learn
  • Insured custodial wallets
  • 0.40-0.60% trading fees — above industry average
  • Customer support can be slow during peak times
Our take: Best first exchange. Start here, learn the basics, then migrate to Kraken for lower fees as you grow.
