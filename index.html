EtherVault

DeFi Wallet & Staking
4.6 ★
A non-custodial wallet with built-in staking and DeFi access. The interface makes it easy for newcomers to earn yield without navigating complex DeFi protocols. Multi-chain support covers all major networks.
  • Non-custodial — you own your keys

Staking Platform
4.1 ★
Purpose-built staking platform supporting 15+ proof-of-stake assets. Offers both flexible and locked staking with transparent APY rates. The dashboard clearly shows earnings over time, which is great for tracking.

  • No minimum stake for flexible options

  • Locked periods can't be early-withdrawn

ChainSwap DEX

Decentralized Exchange
4.4 ★
A cross-chain DEX that lets you swap assets across 20+ blockchains without bridging manually. Liquidity is deep on major pairs, and slippage is consistently low. The bridge aggregator finds the cheapest route automatically.
  • Cross-chain swaps without manual bridging
  • Low slippage on major trading pairs
  • Open-source contracts, regularly audited
  • Gas fees on Ethereum L1 can be high
  • Smaller altcoin selection than CEXs

PayCoin

Crypto Payments

A crypto payment processor for merchants and freelancers. Accept crypto payments and settle in stablecoins or fiat. The checkout widget integrates with major e-commerce platforms in under 10 minutes.
  • 10-minute setup with Shopify, WooCommerce
  • Auto-convert to USDC to avoid volatility
  • Lower fees than traditional payment processors

LendVault

Crypto Lending
4.2 ★
A DeFi lending protocol where you can earn interest on deposits or borrow against your crypto collateral. Rates are competitive and the over-collateralization model keeps the protocol solvent even in volatile markets.
  • Deposit APY up to 8% on stablecoins
  • Over-collateralized loans — no liquidation spirals
  • Insurance fund protects depositors
  • Smart contract risk (audited but not immune)
  • Requires understanding of LTV ratios
Our take: Best for DeFi-savvy users. Start small and understand the risks before depositing significant amounts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is CryptoDealHub affiliated with any platform we review?▼
Yes, we use affiliate links for some platforms. This means we may earn a commission if you sign up through our links, at no extra cost to you. This does not influence our ratings or reviews — we maintain editorial independence and are transparent about our partnerships.
How do you rate crypto platforms?▼
We evaluate platforms across six criteria: fees, security, coin selection, user experience, customer support, and unique features. Each platform is tested hands-on by our team over a minimum of 30 days before we publish a review.
Is crypto investing safe?▼
Crypto is a high-risk asset class. While blockchain technology is secure, the ecosystem carries risks including smart contract bugs, exchange hacks, market volatility, and regulatory changes. Never invest more than you can afford to lose, and always do your own research.
How often are your reviews updated?▼
We review each platform at least quarterly, or immediately when there are significant changes to fees, features, or security incidents. The "last updated" date is shown on every comparison table.
Can I trust your recommendations?▼
We encourage you to use our research as a starting point, not the final word. Cross-reference with other independent sources, read user reviews, and test platforms with small amounts first. We're transparent about our methodology and affiliate relationships.
