Frequently Asked Questions

Is CryptoDealHub affiliated with any platform you review?▼
Yes, we use affiliate links for some platforms. This means we may earn a commission if you sign up through our links, at no extra cost to you. This does not influence our ratings or reviews — we maintain editorial independence.
How do you rate crypto platforms?▼
We evaluate platforms across six criteria: fees, security, coin selection, user experience, customer support, and unique features. Each platform is tested hands-on before we publish a review.
Is crypto investing safe?▼
Crypto is a high-risk asset class. While blockchain technology is secure, the ecosystem carries risks including smart contract bugs, exchange hacks, market volatility, and regulatory changes. Never invest more than you can afford to lose, and always do your own research.
How often are your reviews updated?▼
We review each platform at least quarterly, or immediately when there are significant changes to fees, features, or security incidents. The "last updated" date is shown on every comparison table.
Can I trust your recommendations?▼
We encourage you to use our research as a starting point, not the final word. Cross-reference with other independent sources, read user reviews, and test platforms with small amounts first.
