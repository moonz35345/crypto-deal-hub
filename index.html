Best for DeFi

MetaMask

Browser Wallet & DeFi Gateway · Free
★★★★★ 4.4/5
The most popular Ethereum wallet with 30M+ users. MetaMask connects to virtually every dApp and DeFi protocol. Available as a browser extension and mobile app, it's the essential gateway to the decentralized web.
  • 30M+ active users — the industry standard
  • Connects to virtually every dApp and DeFi protocol
  • Pair with a hardware wallet for enhanced security
  • Free to use, open-source software
  • Vulnerable to phishing if used without hardware signer
  • No built-in fiat on-ramp — need an exchange first

Best Cold Storage

Ledger

Hardware Wallet · $79-$249
★★★★★ 4.8/5
The gold standard for cold storage. Ledger's EAL6+ certified secure element keeps your private keys offline and immune to computer viruses. Supports 5,500+ assets and integrates with MetaMask for DeFi access.
  • EAL6+ certified secure element (same as passports)
  • 5,500+ supported assets across all major chains
  • Integrates with MetaMask for secure DeFi access
  • Bluetooth, USB-C, and NFC connectivity
  • Premium price ($79 Nano S Plus, $249 Ledger Flex)
Our take: Best hardware wallet, period. Worth every cent for securing significant holdings. Pair with MetaMask for the complete DeFi setup.

Best for Beginners

Coinbase

Beginner-Friendly Exchange · Est. 2012
★★★★★ 4.2/5
The easiest on-ramp for complete beginners. Coinbase is publicly traded (NASDAQ: COIN), SOC 2 compliant, keeps 98% of funds in cold storage, and the "Coinbase Earn" program pays you to learn about crypto projects.
  • Easiest fiat on-ramp — bank, card, PayPal
  • Publicly traded company — full financial transparency
  • "Coinbase Earn" pays you small amounts to learn
  • Insured custodial wallets, licensed in all 50 US states
  • 0.40-0.60% fees — above industry average
  • Customer support can be slow during peak times
